Introducing Chimère Pay

A P2P Decentralized Stablecoin Payment System

Think: Google Pay for Stablecoin Payments

Our Goal

We envision to onboard and retain atleast 20% of the Global Population to the Stablecoin Economy.

Why Stablecoins?

Fiat Currencies are centralized silos inhibiting Consumers to consume and Businesses to operate globally whereas Decentralized Cryptocurrencies are too volatile to be accepted as a reliable medium of exchange, thus making Stablecoins the only viable solution for global commerce.

Why Us?

The process of accessing and using Stablecoins via Decentralized Crypto Wallets is too complicated for regular consumers and businesses whereas Centralized Exchanges compromise on the user’s privacy, and ownership, and are not purposely built for payments.

Thus, we are proposing a first of its kind P2P Stablecoin Payment Solution which will ensure a smooth User Experience for regular Consumers and Businesses while maintaining Decentralization.

State of the Art Cloud Encryption

Storing complex recovery phrases and private keys won't be required anymore, they will be safe in your personal cloud drive from the very beginning, locked with a password of your choice, secured by our state of the art encryption.

Simplified Payments

Absurd public keys, complex transaction fee management, multi chain rpc network management would be things of the past. Say hello to Mobile Phone Numbers with Names and Pictures as Payment IDs.

No Transaction limits

Of course.

Smooth Fiat Integration

Just starting out with or feel like exiting the Stablecoin Economy? Our Fiat Integration will ensure your smooth entries and exits.

Your Account, Your Phrases, Your Keys

The best part: all of this: Decentralized. Don't think we are serving you well? Feel free to restrict and export your on chain accounts anytime you wish.
